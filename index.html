📄 Documents to Get from the Seller

Document What to Ask or Check
✅ Original OR (Official Receipt) Ask for the latest receipt from LTO to prove the car is registered.
✅ Original CR (Certificate of Registration) Check that the owner's name and car details match the vehicle being sold.
✅ Notarized Deed of Sale Both you and the seller must sign this, with thumbmarks, and have it notarized.
✅ Three Signatures of the Seller Check that the seller signed on the Deed of Sale, photocopied IDs, and LTO transfer form (like Form 1980).
✅ 2 Valid IDs of the Seller (Photocopy) Ask for clear copies of 2 valid government IDs with signatures.
✅ Ask Where the Car Was First Registered Find out the original LTO branch where the car’s mother file is kept.
